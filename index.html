Agatha Oehlkers, UX Designer

“Good design is a renaissance attitude that combines technology, cognitive science, human need, and beauty to produce something that the world didn’t know it was missing.” (Paola Antonelli - Senior Curator, The Museum of Modern Art, NYC)

Antonelli’s multidisciplinary approach to understanding people and their needs perfectly encapsulates the energy and expertise I bring to my UX work. Being a thoughtful UX researcher/designer requires more than technical skills in HCI and prototyping tools. My original background in social sciences (education and cultural studies) gives me a unique perspective on understanding human behavior and needs.
